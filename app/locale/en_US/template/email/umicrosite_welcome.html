Here's how a Craftsvilla Marketplace process works

  • Step 1
    Buyer pays you through various pre-paid methods like credit card, debit card and internet banking.After processing payment, Craftsvilla fasks you to ship the item.
  • Step 2
    You ship the item and enter shipping details in your Craftsvilla Vendor Account
  • Step 3
    Buyer confirms receipt of the item to Craftsvilla or is entitled to claim refund if item is not delivered or an item is not as decribed.
  • Step 4
    Payment for the order, after deducting 20% commission + service tax, is credited to your bank account through NEFT within 21 working days after shipping the order.

Key Delivery Timelines:

  • If the item is not delivered to the buyer within the timelines, the buyer can initiate a refund request. If you have delivered the item within the timelines, you can appeal against the refund.
  • Ship the item after payment confirmation within: 7 days (national deliveries) / 10 days (international deliveries)
  • Deliver the item after shipping within: 10 days from shipping (national deliveries)
  • Timelines may vary slight for certain categories.
  • You can also request the buyer for a time extension before entering shipping details, if you feel you may not be able to deliver within the original timelines.

Tips:

  • Use a courier service which provides a proper proof of delivery.
  • Retain the proof of delivery for a minimum period of 6 months to help you appeal against a refund request or chargeback by the buyer.
